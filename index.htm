Frequently asked questions

01  What is "Minting an NFT"?

Minting an NFT means being the first buyer. More precisely, the mint of an NFT refers to the activation of the Smart Contracts inscribing the NFT in the Blockchain forever. You will then be able to sell it.

02  What is the purpose of this platform?

03  Why entering in the community?

Lacoste WEB3 community members receive exclusive digital, physical, and experiential privileges as Lacoste pushes the boundaries of fashion and sport by creating a collaborative framework betwwen the community and the brand.

04  How can I mint the Lacoste NFT?

You will have 3 chances to mint the NFT. First chance: be part of the Lacoste List by following WEB3 ecosystem or by being active in our discord community. Second chance to get lucky: win at the premint.xyz raffle. And last chance, if everybody was sleeping on the minting day, you can access the public sale. To be able to mint the NFT and activate any Whitelist you will need at least 0.1 ETH on your Etherum Blockchain Wallet (eg. Metamask) and be here on Thursday 16th June at 12:12 pm CET.

05  What is Ethereum and why did we chose this blockchain ?

Ethereum is a permissionless blockchain accessible to everyone. This chain requires holding Ether or $ETH, the native token of this blockchain, in order to pay transactions and register them in the ledger thanks to the miners' work. Ethereum is the second most secure blockchain in the world, behind Bitcoin. As soon as a user wants to initiate a transaction on Ethereum, he has to pay the network fees, more or less significant depending on the blockchain and the level of traffic on the network at a specific time. These fees are variable and serve to pay miners for their validation work.

06  With which cryptocurrency can I buy a Lacoste NFT?

To acquire a Lacoste NFT, you are asked to pay the sum of $0.08 ETH per NFT, excluding network fees. The payment is made in $ETH when you mint on our website; similarly to OpenSea or LooksRare where you are asked to pay in $ETH to purchase an item on the secondary market.

07  Where should I store my ETH to be ready to mint?

You have to store your ETH on a Metamask-type wallet (or TrustWallet, Coinbase Wallet, Frame, etc.), namely a wallet for which you alone hold the private key. Note that it will not be possible to mint an NFT if your ETH is stored on an exchange platform such as Binance or Coinbase. If your ETH is on such a platform, you will need to send it to your Metamask wallet so that you can later connect your wallet to the site and be able to mint.

08  How to create my Metamask wallet?

First, visit https://metamask.io/ and download the extension to your browser. Then, set up your wallet by selecting "Start" and then "Create Wallet". Create a password and carefully write down the 12 words that will be provided to you on several pieces of paper: this is your private key, extremely confidential and sensitive : a user who has these words can take control of your wallet and access your funds. Never write down these 12 words on a digital medium (mobile, PC, tablet, cloud) connected to the Internet.

10  How to resell my Lacoste NFT ?

To resell your NFT, go to Opensea or LooksRare, go to your profile (by logging in with your Metamask wallet the NFT you want to sell and select Sell then follow the steps to sell. Once your NFT is on sale, it is visible to all browsers who can buy it directly. If your NFT is sold, you will receive the $ETH directly in your Metamask, minus the platform's fees (OpenSea 2.5% / LooksRare 2% + Lacoste royalties of 4%). Listing your NFT involves Ethereum transaction fees, the cost of which goes exclusively to blockchain validators.
